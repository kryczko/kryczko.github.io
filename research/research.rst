Research
========

Kevin Ryczko is a researcher and machine-learning scientist. He obtained a **Ph.D. in Physics** from University of Ottawa (in collaboration with the National Research Council of Canada and the Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence). Before that he obtained his Masters degree in modelling and computational science and Bachelor degree in Physiscs at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology (UOIT, now Ontario Tech University).
